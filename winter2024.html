About

As the official student organization of the economics department, we bring to you themed events and workshops surrounding career exploration, interviewing and resumes, networking and mentorship, and more for you to view a snapshot of everything UES can contribute towards your undergraduate experience.

In order to enroll, participate in our open-invite externship which spans from Week 2 to Week 4, presenting undergrads with the forum to explore their passion and a community to explore it with. With the perfect combination of work and play, these workshops and social events will provide a balanced platform for the all-around university experience we have all missed!

Note: recruitment and externship information will be continuously updated with more events and details as we near the beginning of fall quarter 2023. The minimum attendance requirements will also be decided on and announced at a later date.

The completion of this exciting externship will automatically enroll you as a general member of UES with access to all of our future professional development events and activities, including exclusive access to our esteemed alumni network and faculty. Additionally, completing this prerequisite recruitment event allows you to apply for committee member positions and be a part of the team, where you can help the organization prepare future events!

This program aims to unite undergraduates in their passion for economics through fun-filled activities to create deep and meaningful networks throughout our community.
