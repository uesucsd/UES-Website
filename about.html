About Us


We are the official student organization of the UCSD Economics Department for undergraduate students. With the support of Dr. Melissa Famulari, UES was founded in 2005 with the hopes of establishing a stronger network among all undergraduate economics students by using resources and connecting faculty from the Economics Department. Since its founding, UES has been expanding and evolving to new heights. We provide the resources necessary to prepare our members for embarking on a career in the business world or graduate school through various events and workshops. Students have the opportunity to network with alumni, corporate representatives, graduate students, UCSD faculty, and each other while being a part of an organization designed to enhance the educational and social experience of all interested students at UCSD!






How do I join?

During Fall Quarter, we host our Externship, where you can become a general member by joining our events throughout the three weeks. As a general member, you can have access to all of our future professional development events and activities, including exclusive access to our esteemed alumni network and faculty. Furthermore, you can also apply to become a committee member, where you can help organize events and contribute to the organization. If you do not complete our Externship, you can still join our seminar business events where anyone can attend. Our Externship and seminar events are open to ALL majors at UCSD. To be notified of our upcoming events, simply follow us on Instagram.
